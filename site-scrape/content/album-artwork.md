## Album Artwork

Paul Beveridge.

### Matt Hires

After seeing my unique approach to astrophotography, singer-songwriter Matt Hires approached me to create the photography and design for his latest record "American Wilderness".

We spent an evening in Rocky Mountain National Park shooting expansive long exposure landscapes and unique "astro portraits".

### More Artwork

Varies covers for bands including The Wild After, Leash of Foxes, Racing on the Sun and Ian Stewart.
