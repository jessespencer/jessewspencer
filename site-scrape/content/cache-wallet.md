Visual Design


Interface Design


Prior to Bitcoin's explosive success, Cache Wallet was a pioneer in the digital currency space. The team developed custom hardware devices that allowed users to securely store their bitcoins locally. These devices synced with the mobile app, enabling users to send peer-to-peer payments globally.


Drawing inspiration from science fiction interfaces, the design embodies the product's approach: a futuristic reinvention of financial interaction.
